## About Me

- I like building small, cozy projects that feel gentle to use and quietly well-made
- Most of what I do comes from curiosity — I pick up little ideas, play with them, and slowly turn them into something real

- I'm currently a CS student at SFU, still figuring things out one step at a time
    - learning by building instead of overthinking
    - getting more comfortable problem-solving and structure
    - occasionally getting stuck, then unstuck, then stuck again

- Lately, I’ve been spending time on:
    - 🧩 small algorithm projects (and trying to actually understand them, not just make them work)
    - 📱 simple apps and interfaces that feel clean and calm
    - 🌿 experimenting with little tools, scripts, and ideas just for fun

- I really enjoy:
    - soft visuals and thoughtful details
    - things that feel simple, but are built with care
    - making something small feel *just right*

- Most of the time, I'm just quietly building, learning, and improving a little bit each day
- Usually somewhere in Vancouver time, with a warm drink nearby and too many tabs open

> I like things that look gentle on the outside and feel solid underneath.
